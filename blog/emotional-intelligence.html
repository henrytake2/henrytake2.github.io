Emotional intelligence

What is emotional-intelligence?

Emotional and social skills that influence the way we express ourselves, develop and maintain social relationships, and cope with challenges. identify and regulate one's emotions and understand the emotions the others.

How different is it to IQ?

IQ tests measure your ability to solve problems, use logic, and grasp or communicate complex ideas. EQ tests measure your ability to recognize emotion in yourself and others, and to use that awareness to guide your decisions.

Why is emotional intelligence important?

EQ is important because it helps you to build relationships, reduce team stress, defuse conflict and improve job satisfaction. And its also important for personal growth.
