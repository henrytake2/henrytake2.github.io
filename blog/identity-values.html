Identity and Values

Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. The decision you made and the consequences of the action you took

I was serving time in jail when i came across a course called Take2. They teach prisoners how to code. Before joining this course i didn't know where i was heading in life. I thought i was going to go back doing what i was doing before going to prison once released. But not long after joining Take2 i realised that this was something that i enjoy doing and saw that it was an opportunity for me to do something good with my life. So coding has changed my life.

Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity

Im from Tahiti and Cook islands. My life experiences being a Pacific islander has taught me to be respectful to others, be humble, caring and giving and being family orientated.

Describe your strengths and limitations.

My strengths are that i am determined, resilient compassionate. I also learned about having a growth mindset beleiving that my basic abilities can be developed through hardwork and dedication. That is something that i have struggled with for a while now. beleiving that im not good enough or smart enough to do something. So developing a growth mindset has help me.
